Hatdaggidy diggidy dog dog

Description

In the 21st century, dog meat is consumed in China, Nigeria, Switzerland, and Vietnam, and it is eaten or is legal to be eaten in other countries throughout the world.

  • knife
  • frying pan
  • meat grinder

Steps

  1. Buy dog meat
  2. mince dog meat and debone
  3. put into meat grinder/food processor
  4. salt and mold into hotdog-like shape
  5. Fry hotdog and don't forget to season
  6. Serve.
